Household Of Faith

Household of Faith Ministry started in June 2014 after the Lord Jesus Christ placed a ministry call upon pastor Philomena Ikonagbon. Pastor Ikonagbon took the step of faith and answered the call, not knowing where she was being led but being assured that God who called her was in control.

Acts 13:2 says, As they ministered to the Lord, and fasted, the Holy Ghost said, Separate me Barnabas and Saul for the work whereunto I have called them. We know we are called to a specific work and will empower others to lay hold on the promises of God, knowing he is faithful s promises.
