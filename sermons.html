"Too Quick To Quit" - Sunday 25th March 2018

2 Kings 13:14-19
Mark 7:25-29
Mark 10:46-52

When we are too quick to quit we run the risk of missing out on the purpose for which God has called us.

"For the vision is yet for an appointed time, but at the end it shall speak, and not lie: though it tarry, wait for it; because it will surely come, it will not tarry."
- Habakkuk 2:3

If it feels as though all hope is lost and everyone is telling you to give it a rest — dig deeper, pusher harder and scream louder; your breakthrough may be just around the corner.

Our prayer is that God will bless us all with patience to wait on Him and perseverance that we may keep going until we achieve the victory God has already preordained.

Have a blessed week!

"How Do You Respond?" - Sunday 18th March 2018

2 Kings 4:18-37
2 Timothy 1:7
Hebrews 11:6

When we are in pieces, the presence of God is where we find our peace.

Our trials enable us to discover a multifaceted God. We will never understand God the Healer until we need healing and we will never understand God the Comforter until we need comforting.

It is very easy to serve God faithfully while facing no challenges, how do you respond when your world seems to be falling apart?

Our prayer is that God will bless us all with a faith that proclaims “It is well!” in the face of adversity.

Have a blessed week.

"The Influence of a Mother" - Sunday 11th March 2018

John 2:1-11
Genesis 27:1, 5-10
Proverbs 31:30

As a mother it is important to understand the influence you have over your children.

A child who grows up seeing their mother enthusiastically serve God will likely approach their own service with that same unshakeable joy. In contrast, a child who grows up seeing their mother treat God like an afterthought will be less likely to prioritise God above all things.

"Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not depart from it."
- Proverbs 22:6

The way you raise your children has the potential to make or break the next generation. We pray that God will bless every mother with the wisdom to raise a generation of children who will stand firm in truth, sharing God's everlasting love to the ends of the earth.

Happy Mother's Day!

Have a blessed week.
