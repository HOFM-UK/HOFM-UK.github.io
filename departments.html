Choir

"Enter into His gates with thanksgiving,
And into His courts with praise.
Be thankful to Him, and bless His name."
- Psalms 100:4

Household of Faith believe in Revelation 4:11 which says that we are created for God's pleasure to give Him glory, honour and to say how worthy he is to be praised. Hence, we joyfully enter his gates with thanksgiving in our heart and courts with praise at all times.

If you are gifted and talented in music please feel free to join us as we serve in the house of the lord.
