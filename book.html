Earthly Career & Heavenly Calling

To some there is no such thing as heaven; everything simply starts and ends here on earth. However, as believers we are confident that there is a reward at the end of this earthly journey. Unfortunately, not all are aware of this reality. This book illustrates the author's thoughts on this subject. It contains an account of the inspiration of the Holy Spirit and provides an analysis of the treasures laid up in heaven. In conclusion, it re-emphasises the need to focus more on the heavenly assignment which is eternal rather than fleeting earthly gains.

As you peruse these pages you will discover the importance of living life in Christ and how to enjoy the rewards that await every Christian. You will also come across useful steps on how to enrich your heavenly account; steps that work through faith. 'Earthly Career and Heavenly Calling' is intended for both the believer and non-believer alike.
