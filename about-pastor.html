Pastor Philomena Ikonagbon

Pastor Philomena Ikonagbon founded and successfully led the ‘Women of Faith Ministry UK’ for over a decade. She is also the founder of ‘Faith Family Network’, a community based group which brought Christian families together, instilling family values as demonstrated in the bible. After leading these ministries for many years, pastor Philomena answered the call of God upon her life and founded ‘Household of Faith Ministry, City of Truth’ in 2014.

Known for her sincere love for God and a strong desire to please Him, pastor Philomena believes in seeking and allowing the will of the Father to prevail in her life. She believes in the demonstration of the power of God and sees His manifestation on a constant basis.

A powerful prayer warrior and a respected teacher of God‘s word, pastor Philomena is passionate about the salvation of the souls of men and encourages everyone to develop a personal relationship with Christ as their Lord and Savior.

Pastor Philomena holds an LLB (Hon) in Law and Graduate Diploma in Legal Practice. She lives, works and pastors in London where she is supported by her husband, Osaretin and their two children.
