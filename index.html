Ministry

We are part of the family of church endeavouring to promote the truth of the gospel to all we come in contact with. A place to meet and encounter God in a supernatural way.

Mission

To impact nations and our world with the truth in God's world. Setting free as many in preparation for the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ through evangelism, teaching, discipleship and missions.

Message

You are welcome to contact us or join us at any of our service. We are confident that you will be blessed. We promote the Christian faith in all we do. We are about our father's business promoting the saving grace of our Lord and saviour Jesus Christ.
