Wednesday 5th December 2018

Day 3: Praying For Marriages, Loving Homes and March 2019

  • Written by Pastor Philomena
  • Tagged as EOY Prayer and Fasting

God instituted marriage at the very beginning when He created Adam and Eve. God’s purpose for marriage is to reflect His image, reproduce and reign together with Him.

Pray that we will be at one with our spouse and with God. Pray for cooperation, unity and encouragement  

"And the Lord God caused a deep sleep to fall on Adam, and he slept; and He took one of his ribs, and closed up the flesh in its place. Then the rib which the Lord God had taken from man He made into a woman, and He brought her to the man. And Adam said: “This is now bone of my bones and flesh of my flesh; she shall be called Woman, because she was taken out of Man.” Therefore a man shall leave his father and mother and be joined to his wife, and they shall become one flesh."
- Genesis 2: 21-24

Pray that no one shall be barren in HOF. Pray also that our children will not be barren when they get married. Pray that our marriages, homes and those of our loved ones, friends and family shall be fruitful.

"No one shall suffer miscarriage or be barren in your land; I will fulfill the number of your days."
- Exodus 23:26

Our struggle is not against our spouse but against satan and his agents who want us to believe that our spouse is the problem. Pray against the enemy of our marriages, against divorce and separation.

"For we do not wrestle against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this age, against spiritual hosts of wickedness in the heavenly places."
- Ephesians 6:12

Pray that our children will find their right partners. That they will not marry another person spouse. They will only marry those that the Lord has ordained for them. Pray that the Lord will grant them wisdom at the time of finding their soulmates. Above all, pray that they will obtain favour from the Lord. Pray for stable and loving homes for our youth when they get married.

"He who finds a wife finds a good thing, and obtains favor from the Lord."
- Proverbs 18:22

Pray for wholeness; spiritually and mentally. Pray that His peace will be with us every day in March 2019 and every other month in the year 2019. Pray that we will not be anxious for anything but that our minds will stay on Him throughout the year 2019.

"Be anxious for nothing, but in everything by prayer and supplication, with thanksgiving, let your requests be made known to God; and the peace of God, which surpasses all understanding, will guard your hearts and minds through Christ Jesus."
- Philippians 4:6-7

Tuesday 4th December 2018

Day 2: Praying For February 2019

  • Written by Pastor Philomena
  • Tagged as EOY Prayer and Fasting

Decree unto the gates of February 2019, to lift up its head that I, (your name), may enter into God’s uncommon favour, in the name of Jesus. Let's Pray and cover each day February 2019 and declaring God's plan be established for us all.

Declare peace in our families, peace over our children, financial blessings for all in HOF and favour of God in all our endeavours in 2019.

“Death and life are in the power of the tongue, and those who love it will eat its fruit.”
- Proverbs 18:21

“You will also declare a thing, and it will be established for you; So light will shine on your ways.”
- Job 22:28

Pray that our children would not have to carry their dreams long before God will cause a manifestation of His promises in their lives.

"''Shall I bring to the time of birth, and not cause delivery?' says the Lord. 'Shall I who cause delivery shut up the womb?' says your God.”
- Isaiah 66:9

Pray and declare that in 2019 we shall be found of the Lord and our head shall be anointed with His holy oil and through our lives the hand of Lord shall be established and His hands shall strengthen us in Jesus name.

“I have found My servant David; With My holy oil I have anointed him, with whom My hand shall be established; Also My arm shall strengthen him."
- Psalm 89:20-21

Let’s Pray that whatever sin limited us from achieving God’s purpose in 2018, that God will remake us into worthy vessels so as to fulfil His purpose in 2019.

"And the vessel that he made of clay was marred in the hand of the potter; so he made it again into another vessel, as it seemed good to the potter to make."
- Jeremiah 18:4

Yes February 2019 is our set time of favour, in our homes, finances, our work places, in ministry and in whatever area. Therefore, pray that God’s unmerited favour from heaven, will locate us by fire even though we don’t deserve it, in the name of Jesus.

“You will arise and have mercy on Zion; For the time to favor her, yes, the set time, has come.”
- Psalm 102:13

Pray that the end of our 2019 will be far better that the beginning.

"The end of a thing is better than its beginning;"
- Ecclesiastes 7:8a

"You crown the year with Your goodness, and Your paths drip with abundance."
- Psalm 65:11

God bless and give us all enablement to wait upon Him.


"The Lord will open to you His good treasure, the heavens, to give the rain to your land in its season, and to bless all the work of your hand. You shall lend to many nations, but you shall not borrow."
- Deuteronomy 28:12

"Then this Daniel distinguished himself above the governors and satraps, because an excellent spirit was in him; and the king gave thought to setting him over the whole realm."
- Daniel 6:3

"Commit your works to the Lord, and your thoughts will be established."
- Proverbs 16:3.

Monday 3rd December 2018

Day 1: Start With A Heart Of Gratitude.

  • Written by Pastor Philomena
  • Tagged as EOY Prayer and Fasting

Glorify His Name for keeping you and your loved ones alive and well throughout 2018. Have a heart of gratitude and bless His holy Name. Enter His gate with thanksgiving and His courts with praise. Spend the day praising God for His loving kindness and His faithfulness in all areas. Focus on His goodness throughout the day.

Pray for Joy unspeakable all year round in our homes/families and our church. Pray over each day of the month of January 2019. Declaring over your life, your family and His church that we will experience His unspeakable joy all year round. Please pray as you are led throughout the day.

"It is good to give thanks to the Lord, and to sing praises to Your name, O Most High. To declare Your lovingkindness in the morning, and Your faithfulness every night, on an instrument of ten strings, on the lute, and on the harp, with harmonious sound. For You, Lord, have made me glad through Your work; I will triumph in the works of Your hands. O Lord, how great are Your works! Your thoughts are very deep. A senseless man does not know, nor does a fool understand this. When the wicked spring up like grass, and when all the workers of iniquity flourish, it is that they may be destroyed forever. But You, Lord, are on high forevermore."
- Psalms 92:1-8

"Oh come, let us sing to the Lord! Let us shout joyfully to the Rock of our salvation. Let us come before His presence with thanksgiving; let us shout joyfully to Him with psalms. For the Lord is the great God, and the great King above all gods. In His hand are the deep places of the earth; the heights of the hills are His also. The sea is His, for He made it; and His hands formed the dry land. Oh come, let us worship and bow down; let us kneel before the Lord our Maker. For He is our God, and we are the people of His pasture, and the sheep of His hand. Today, if you will hear His voice:"
- Psalms 95:1-7

"Make a joyful shout to the Lord, all you lands! Serve the Lord with gladness; come before His presence with singing. Know that the Lord, He is God; it is He who has made us, and not we ourselves; we are His people and the sheep of His pasture. Enter into His gates with thanksgiving, and into His courts with praise. Be thankful to Him, and bless His name. For the Lord is good; His mercy is everlasting, and His truth endures to all generations."
- Psalms 100:1-5

"You will show me the path of life; in Your presence is fullness of joy; at Your right hand are pleasures forevermore."
- Psalms 16:11

"Therefore with joy you will draw water from the wells of salvation."
- Isaiah 12:3

May the Lord Almighty strengthen you for the days ahead.
