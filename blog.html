Tuesday 4th December 2018

Day 2: Praying For February 2019

  • Written by Pastor Philomena
  • Tagged as EOY Prayer and Fasting

Decree unto the gates of February 2019, to lift up its head that I, (mention your name) may enter into God’s uncommon favour, in the name of Jesus. Let's Pray and cover each day February 2019 and declaring God's plan be established for us all.
Declare peace in our families, peace over our children, financial blessings for all in HOF and favour of God in all our endeavours in 2019.
“Death and life are in the power of the tongue, and those who love it will eat its fruit.” - Proverbs 18:21
“You will also declare a thing, and it will be established for you; So light will shine on your ways.” - Job 22:28

Pray that our children would not have to carry their dreams long before God will cause a manifestation of His promises in their lives.
"'' Shall I bring to the time of birth, and not cause delivery?' says the Lord. 'Shall I who cause delivery shut up the womb?' says your God.” - Isaiah 66:9

Pray and declare that in 2019 we shall be found of the Lord and our head shall be anointed with His holy oil and through our lives the hand of Lord shall be established and His hands shall strengthen us in Jesus name.
“I have found My servant David; With My holy oil I have anointed him, with whom My hand shall be established; Also My arm shall strengthen him." - Psalm 89:20-21

Let’s Pray that whatever sin limited us from achieving God’s purpose in 2018, that God will REMAKE us into worthy vessels so as to fulfil His purpose in 2019.
"And the vessel that he made of clay was marred in the hand of the potter; so he made it again into another vessel, as it seemed good to the potter to make." - Jeremiah 18:4

Yes February 2019 is our set time of favour, in our homes, finances, our work places, in ministry and in whatever area. Therefore, pray that God’s unmerited favour from heaven, will locate us by fire even though we don’t deserve it, in the name of Jesus.
“You will arise and have mercy on Zion; For the time to favor her, yes, the set time, has come.” - Psalm 102:13

Pray that the end of our 2019 will be far better that the beginning.
"The end of a thing is better than its beginning;" - Ecclesiastes 7:8a
"You crown the year with Your goodness, and Your paths drip with abundance." - Psalm 65:11

God bless and give us all enablement to wait upon Him.

"The Lord will open to you His good treasure, the heavens, to give the rain to your land in its season, and to bless all the work of your hand. You shall lend to many nations, but you shall not borrow." - Deuteronomy 28:12

"Then this Daniel distinguished himself above the governors and satraps, because an excellent spirit was in him; and the king gave thought to setting him over the whole realm." - Daniel 6:3

"Commit your works to the Lord, and your thoughts will be established." - Proverbs 16:3

Monday 3rd December 2018

Day 1: Start With A Heart Of Gratitude.

  • Written by Pastor Philomena
  • Tagged as EOY Prayer and Fasting

Glorify His Name for keeping you and your loved ones alive and well throughout 2018. Have a heart of gratitude and bless His holy Name. Enter His gate with thanksgiving and His courts with praise. Spend the day praising God for His loving kindness and His faithfulness in all areas. Focus on His goodness throughout the day.

Pray for Joy unspeakable all year round in our homes/families and our church. Pray over each day of the month of January 2019. Declaring over your life, your family and His church that we will experience His unspeakable joy all year round. Please pray as you are led throughout the day.

  • Psalms 92:1-8
  • Psalms 95:1-7
  • Psalms 100:1-5
  • Psalms 16:11
  • Isaiah 12:3

May the Lord Almighty strengthen you for the days ahead.
