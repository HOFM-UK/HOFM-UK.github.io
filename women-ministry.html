WOMEN OF FAITH MINISTRY is now an arm of HOUSEHOLD OF FAITH - CITY OF TRUTH

This ministry was initially founded in 2004 as a women's prayer group following the leading of the Holy Spirit. This was as a result of Pastor Philomena's heart cry and desire for God to intervene in her own personal life challenges. Pastor Philomena was led by the Holy Spirit to begin to lead other women in intercession and prayer. The Holy Spirit ministered to her that as she begins to lead or intercede with others and support them through their own personal challenges, God will eventually take care of Pastor Philomena's personal needs. Ever since she obeyed that call, God being a covenant keeping God has indeed proven to be the Almighty God who honours His word more than His Name

We believe that God has put in each woman a gift that is needed in the world and in this generation. Therefore, Women of Faith Ministry has a vision of empowering every woman to stir-up that God-given gifts within without fear - 2 Timothy 1:6-7 says, ' 6 Therefore I remind you to stir up the gift of God which is in you through the laying on of my hands. 7 For God has not given us a spirit of fear, but of power and of love and of a sound mind.

We believe that prayer works and our obligation is to simply trust God and His Word. As we do so in faith and obedience, we will receive the manifestation of His power and promises in due season if we do not loose heart. Our aim is to teach every woman how to use the Word of God to achieve tremendous result in the place of prayer. The promise we have in God that He hears us when we pray is found in 1 John 5:14 which says '14 Now this is the confidence that we have in Him, that if we ask anything according to His will, He hears us'. We therefore have that assurance in God and His Word that if we dare to have faith even as small as a mustard seed, that mountain will eventually move.

Our goal is to

  • Impact as many women positively around the world starting with those in our community
  • Reach out to those facing challenging situations in their personal lives and provide support
  • Encourage spiritual growth through the Word of God and prayer
  • Promote personal development and leadership skills
  • Contribute to the general welfare of the home, community and the world at large

Our monthly meetings provides us with opportunity to worship God, pray, encourage one another with His Word and share great testimonies of God's faithfulness. It is a place for us to connect with one another and simply worship God in the beauty of His holiness.

  • Regular women's monthly meetings every first Saturday of the month
  • Women's Mini Night vigil every quarter
  • Discipleship/mentoring of new members and daughters
  • Evangelism/outreach within the community i.e. supporting Food Bank project
  • Mother's Day celebration
  • Women's conference
  • Christmas show of love
